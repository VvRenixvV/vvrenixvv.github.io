About me

Enthusing people with "something" inspiring and creative has always been a thrill for me. In search of that "something", I found myself studying Software Engineering at the University of Sheffield. It's a bit selfish, but I've realized developing beautiful and useful software is my way of experiencing the thrill. I've always wanted to be inventive but also wasn't afraid of applying myself when building that "something", so software and it's infinite potentials is the perfect thing for me.

Below are a few sentences, I would use to describe myself:

  • I believe being able to adapt to my circumstances is my greatest strength.
  • I really really enjoy teamwork, for me it is top 3 things I love about building software.
  • I am a "forever student" of anything that stands outside the box.
  • My friends would call me "charismatic", "wierd" and sometimes "over-optimistic" but they won't call me to dinner.
