Block Quest

Play on Desktop

3D | Puzzle | Strategy | Tilemap

This was my second completed game in Unity. It is highly inspired by the game Bloxorz, which I used to play as a kid on Miniclip in the browser. Between the satisfying controls, challenging levels and the unique atmoshphere in the original game, I used to forget the real world for hours. I knew I had to attempt to build this by myself, in my journey to learning Unity.
Nothing beats the original, but I have managed to successfully replicate most of the game elements, as well adding my own little castle/tower/medieval setting to the game. Game elements include (ref: Bloxorz Wiki):

  • Normal tiles - standard tile
  • Brige Tiles - a tile that opens/closes when their switch is toggled.
  • Round Switch - switch that activates when any part of the block lands on it
  • Cross Switch - switch that activates only when the block stands on it vertically
  • Fragile Tile - tile that breaks if the block stands on it vertically
